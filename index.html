About me

Hi! My name is April Chang, and I am currently a junior Computer Science Games major at USC, graduating May 2022.
I enjoy developing games because it incorporates two things that I am most passionate about - programming and art.
With my multidisciplinary skills, I am able to take on different roles in teams including programmer, level designer, UI designer, character artist, and a problem solver.

The Trials of Snowshoe Thompson (Demo)

Previous
Next

Description:

The Trials of Snowshoe Thompson is a friction-focused skiing game about the life of Snowshoe Thompson, an immigrant finding community set in the Sierra Nevada mountains of 1856.
We have a team of more than 20 people working on this year-long project, which will be showcased in USC Game Expo in May 2021.

Description:

ShoPandemic is a local co-op game where you and your friend try to buy groceries while social distancing and keep everyone safe during a pandemic! This is a two person project over the course of a semester, and we plan to make more levels and launch it on Steam.
